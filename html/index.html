Welcome to FOSSCOMM 2024

FOSSCOMM (Free and Open Source Software Communities Meeting) is a Greek conference aiming at Open Source enthusiasts, developers, and communities. It is addressed to everyone interested in free and open source software, open data, open hardware, and digital rights. The conference brings together the communities of different FOSS projects (e.g. Mozilla, Fedora, Wikimedia, Gnome, KDE, LibreOffice, etc.), as well as people from the academic and business sector.

Stay tuned for more information about the conference schedule, speakers, and other details.
